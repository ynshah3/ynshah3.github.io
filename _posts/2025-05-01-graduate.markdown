This award recognizes outstanding graduate students who have demonstrated excellence in academic
achievement, leadership, and service within the Stanford community. This award honors
students who have made meaningful contributions to fostering an inclusive and supportive
environment and creating opportunities for others. Recipients may be recognized for their
impact as role models and leaders through academic excellence, mentorship, and community
engagement—whether within Stanford or through outreach beyond.
